You are a internediate arabic student making a slideshow for their arabic assignment and must write out how your hypothetical experience was if you were to get accepted to work for a couple months as an engineer in the UAE 
Create an outline for a visual presentation about this Arab country in which you present information that explains the following:

1. What are a couple of things you plan to buy from an Arabic Souk in this country? Did you have to bargain to get a better price? What did you buy? What were the traditional souvenirs in this country (Module 1)
2. What are some popular dishes of the area and what ingredients did they include? Make a recommendation to a person who might visit the area on what to eat. (Module 1)
3. Describe an archeological site that you visited and any tourist attraction places in this country. ( Module 1)
4. Explain how you had to eat healthy during this visit, though Arab hospitality had to put more pounds on your weight! You knew that it is not culturally appropriate to refuse a 2nd or a 3rd serving! Were you exercising? What kind of sports did you practice? (Module 2)
5. What kinds of music and songs were you able to listen to? Who is the most famous singer in this country? What is the traditional music instrument there? (Module 2)
6. How did you communicate with your family back in the US? Did you use your cell phone? Did you send Emails? (Module3)
7. During your visit did you get sick/injured what was the health problem? Explain were you had pain and how you got better? Did you try some herbal medicine? (Module4)
8. Describe the weather at the time of your visit (share the season as well) and state any natural environment that you liked: mountains- sea - ocean- desert - trees in this country( Module 4)
9. How could you describe your experience from this trip? State an exclamatory Arabic style using appropriate Grammar to wrap up your trip. ( Module 4)

- Each of the numbered items requires **at least 3 complete sentences** and all of the grammar points from the course should be present in the project.

- Include **6 images** with proper citations. Include the author, title, web link, and license. If the images are your own work, **please offer citations** for those as well.
