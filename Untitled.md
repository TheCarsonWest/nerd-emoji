## My Favorite Arabic Artist: Fairuz (فيروز)

**1.  Recording in Arabic:**

*   "أُحب فيروز كثيرًا. صوتها جميل جداً. أنا أُفضل أغانيها القديمة." 
    *   Translation: "I love Fairuz very much. Her voice is very beautiful. I prefer her old songs."
    *   Latin Transliteration: "Uhibbu Fairuz kathiran. Sautuha jamil jaddan. Ana ufaḍḍil ʾaghaniha al-qadīmah."

**3.  Reciting Two Lines:**

*   "يا طيبة يا بلادي يا حبيبي يا بلادي" 
    *   Translation: "Oh, how good you are, my country, my love, oh, my country"
    *   Latin Transliteration: "Ya ṭayibah ya biladi ya ḥabibi ya biladi"

*   "يا ريتني طير بأطير على جناح الهوى"
    *   Translation: "I wish I was a bird, to fly on the wings of love"
    *   Latin Transliteration: "Ya raytani ṭayr biʾaṭīr ʿala janāḥ al-hawā"
